Week 1
Mar 13

Meet Your AI: ChatGPT, Copilot & Claude

A tasting platter of prompting, creativity, meeting support, and setup basics.

Expected outcome: Participants can confidently configure AI tools, write structured prompts, and use AI for meetings, creativity, and daily productivity tasks.
Section 1: AI Settings & Prompting Basics (15 min)Walk through ChatGPT settings, memory, and custom instructions. Demonstrate live while participants follow and test.
Section 2: Creative AI Use Case (15 min)Demonstrate image generation or style transformation. Participants try their own version and experiment.
Section 3: Copilot for Meetings (15 min)Generate meeting notes and action items from a recording. Participants follow using a sample or real meeting.
Section 4: Prompt Like a Pro (15 min)Practice rewriting weak prompts into strong ones and test results live.

Create Your AI Assistant

A practical follow-along session where you build a chatbot connected to company knowledge, then adapt it into a useful business assistant for a real scenario.

Expected outcome: Participants can build a functional chatbot connected to company knowledge and adapt it to a real business use case.
Section 1: Build a Copilot Agent Connected to Company Knowledge (15 min)Watch a live demo of building a Copilot agent that answers questions using information from a finance folder in SharePoint.
Section 2: Build a ChatGPT Agent for a Specific Task (15 min)Watch a live demo of building a ChatGPT agent that generates a document in a specific format based on an uploaded file.
Section 3: Build Your Own Agent (30 min)Use the second half of the session to build something yourself on the spot, test it, and ask questions as you go.

AI Security & Demystify Popular AI Tools

Learn safe usage, account differences, and how to judge new tools with confidence.

Expected outcome: Participants understand what data is safe to share, how to use work versus personal accounts responsibly, and how to evaluate new AI tools critically.
Section 1: What Is Safe to Share (15 min)Walk through practical examples of safe and unsafe inputs. Participants review their own usage.
Section 2: Personal vs Work Accounts (15 min)Demonstrate differences in settings and risks. Participants check their configurations.
Section 3: Other Popular AI Tools Overview (15 min)NotebookLM, Gemini, and why caution is required.
Section 4: Open Security Q&A (15 min)Bring real scenarios and questions. Clarify guardrails and best practices.
